Section 1: My Biography

Samiul Azam received M.Sc. degree in Computer Science from the University of Calgary, Canada in July 2017. Before that, he received M.Sc degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), Bangladesh in 2014, and B.Sc. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST), Bangladesh in 2010. His research interests are focused on biometrics, image processing, machine learning, pattern recognition and data analysis.
